## Design Context

### Users

Neuronmeet is primarily for remote teams using it for work meetings. The core job is to get people into meetings quickly with minimal friction, then keep the in-call experience dependable and easy to manage.

### Brand Personality

Professional, trustworthy, and calm. The interface should reduce uncertainty, feel reliable under pressure, and avoid visual choices that read as heavy dark-mode gamer aesthetics.

### Aesthetic Direction

Prefer a polished, modern collaboration product feel over a flashy or entertainment-oriented one. Visual decisions should support speed, clarity, and credibility first.

### Design Principles

- Make join and start actions obvious within seconds.
- Reduce friction before and during meetings.
- Use visual hierarchy to communicate confidence, not decoration.
- Keep the experience professional without becoming sterile.
- Avoid trendy dark-mode patterns that make the product feel generic or gamer-oriented.
